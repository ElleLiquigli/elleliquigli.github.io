Steven Universe Fandom

Hello, world! Steven Universe is the queerest and most wholesome televsion show in the world.
You can't help but fall in love with the selfless and brave gems who are constantly defending the Earth from attack... and sometimes causing mayhem themselves.
The love and friendship shared by these gems and humans will have you wanting to be a Crystal Gem too.

Character Development

Pearl is one of the characters who experiences the greatest development within the show. She breaks my heart.
Amethyst is another character whose growth is so apparent as the show goes on. She becomes so mature!
